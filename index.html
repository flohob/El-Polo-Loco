El Pollo Loco

In the endless expanses of the Mexican desert, Pepe Peligroso, a brave adventurer, faces a plague of wild chickens. Armed with nothing but salsa bottles, he confronts the feathered adversaries. As Pepe battles through the hot sand, he encounters chickens with audacious maneuvers, revealing themselves as "CocoLocos" and "Taco Tornados". Yet, his true adversary awaits at the end of the desert - the largest chicken of them all, feared by everyone as "El Pollo Loco".
